what makes us different!

Chembakavalli Jewellery has been a cornerstone in the industry for the past 50 years, specializing in crafting exquisite silver and gold jewellery.Founded by Kollappan Achari in the 1980's is now thriving with a legacy of half a century, Chembakavalli Jewellery has consistently delivered exceptional craftsmanship and timeless designs that resonate with elegance and sophistication. Their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned them a reputation as a trusted name in the jewellery market.
